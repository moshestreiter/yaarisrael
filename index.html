🌳 Why Miyawaki Forests?

Miyawaki forests are dense, biodiverse, and self-sustaining native ecosystems. They restore degraded land, boost biodiversity, sequester carbon, and reconnect people with nature.

Yaarisrael’s mission is to rewild Israel using Miyawaki forests grown from our own nursery of native trees and plants, specifically adapted to regions like the Beit She’an Valley.
