YaarIsrael: Cultivating Native Forests in Israel

YaarIsrael is dedicated to ecological restoration of the nature of Israel through the planting of ultra-dense, native Miyawaki forests across Israel. We operate our own nursery to grow native tree and plant species for diverse forest ecosystems.

Existing forests in Israel dry out during the summer months becoming vulnerable to natural and terrorist caused fires.

Our solution is to plant forests combining the Miyawaki forestry method in addition to using innovative watering and misting systems to keep the forest ecosystem cool and wet.

Our target markets are national forestry organizations and NGO's in Israel as well as private individuals who would like to sponsor a forest on public or private property in Israel.

There exists other forestry organizations including some Miyawaki foresters in Israel however these are not seen as competition, rather as potential clients.

YaarIsrael will contract to install and maintain natural forests with appropriate irrigation systems, as well as sell surplus native trees and plants wholesale to other forestry companies and gardening centers in the country.

Ecological Layers & Native Species

Canopy Layer

  • Ziziphus spina-christi (שיזף מצוי) – Hardy, drought-tolerant, shade provider
  • Quercus ithaburensis (אלון התבור) – Native oak, strong ecological presence
  • Pistacia atlantica (אלה אטלנטית) – Deep-rooted, keystone tree
  • Tamarix jordanis (תמריר הירדן) – Salt-tolerant tree for valley bottoms

Understory/Sub-Canopy Layer

  • Ceratonia siliqua (חרוב מצוי) – Carob, nitrogen-fixer, edible pods
  • Acacia raddiana (שיטה סלילנית) – Arid-zone nitrogen-fixer

Shrub Layer

  • Retama raetam (רותם המדבר) – White broom, flowering desert shrub
  • Capparis spinosa (צלף קוצני) – Caper bush, edible and iconic
  • Rhamnus palaestinus (אשחר ארץ-ישראלי) – Buckthorn, attracts birds
  • Calicotome villosa (שיזף קוצני) – Thorny shrub for seedling protection

Herbaceous / Groundcover Layer

  • Salvia fruticosa (מרווה משולשת) – Sage, pollinator-attractor
  • Sarcopoterium spinosum (סירה קוצנית) – Soil-regenerating groundcover
  • Stipa capensis (זיפנוצה מדברית) – Perennial bunchgrass
  • Echinops adenocaulos (קיפודן מצוי) – Pollinator thistle with blue blooms

YaarIsrael Business Plan Overview

YaarIsrael operates as a native tree and plant nursery and forest contracting company dedicated to the restoration of Israel’s native ecosystems using the Miyawaki method. Our work focuses on three core areas:

  • Nursery Operations: We grow a large variety of native trees, shrubs, and groundcovers for use in forest restoration projects across Israel.
  • Miyawaki Forest Design & Implementation: We design and plant Miyawaki forests in urban, rural, and degraded areas to promote rapid ecosystem recovery.
  • Long-Term Maintenance: We provide aftercare including irrigation, weeding, and monitoring to ensure forest health.

We partner with local authorities, schools, developers, and conservation organizations to create lush forests teeming with life that serve communities, biodiversity, and climate resilience.
