Phase 1: Seed Procurement & Nursery Establishment

  • Collect and store seeds from native species.
  • Build nursery infrastructure (shadehouse, irrigation, composting).
  • Raise 2,500–3,000 native seedlings in 6 months.

Phase 2: Plant Pilot Miyawaki Forest

  • Select 1-dunam site in the Beit She’an Valley.
  • Plant and mulch native seedlings in dense Miyawaki layout.
  • Maintain for 2–3 years with drip irrigation and organic mulch.

Funding: Grants (KKL-JNF, government), partial contracts, community partners.
